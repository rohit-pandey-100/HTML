Cancer is a group of diseases characterized by the uncontrolled growth and spread of abnormal cells. It can occur almost anywhere in the body, typically forming solid tumors or affecting the blood. If left untreated, these cells can invade surrounding tissues and metastasize to distant organs, which can be fatal
