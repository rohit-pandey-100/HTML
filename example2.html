Emerging Disease Threats in India

🦠Nipah Virus (NiV):

Carried by fruit bats, this pathogen primarily spills over to humans through direct contact with infected animals or consumption of contaminated raw date palm sap. Outbreaks have been contained to regions in Kerala and West Bengal. Early flu-like symptoms quickly progress to severe brain inflammation (encephalitis). Because the case fatality rate ranges from 40% to 75%, health bodies monitor it


⚠Chandipura Virus:

This rare but severe mosquito- and sandfly-borne illness has resurfaced in Gujarat, predominantly affecting children and leading to rapid neurological decline. Health authorities are executing door-to-door screenings and containment drives in affected regions.
