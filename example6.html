🩺 Systemic & Whole-Body Symptoms

Cancer symptoms vary greatly based on the affected organ system, but their primary characteristic is that they are unexplained, persistent, and worsen over time. While most of these symptoms are frequently caused by less serious, non-cancerous conditions, it is crucial to have them evaluated by a physician if they last more than a few weeks.
