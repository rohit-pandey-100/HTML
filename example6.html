Yamaha R2 likely to debut in India on August 27

Yamaha has sent out media invites for a product presentation on 27 August 2026, widely believed to be the launch of the YZF-R2. While the company has not officially confirmed the model, multiple sources point to the R2 as the most likely unveiling. The event is described as focusing on performance and riding excitement, aligning with the R2’s expected positioning above the R15.
